About

Hey there, I'm George. As the motto on the front of my website says, I'm an entrepreneur, technologist, and business person who aspires to take the path less traveled. What that means is that I envision building a future not constrained by the normal 9-5 work-life balance but one that encompasses the malleability of the world in terms of harnessing technological change and encouraging disruption to the way we live.

I've got a background in tech and programming from the early years of my life (starting around age 10) and can write programs in languages such as Ruby and Python. I'm primarily a software developer at heart but have also spent a lot of time in product design, database management, and machine learning/data science. However, as I progressed in life, I realized the need to understand the language of business so I studied business management with a focus on finance and real estate development at the University of Maryland.
